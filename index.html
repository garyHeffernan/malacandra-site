A note on how this firm is run —

One steward, one strategy, one account that stays entirely your own.

Malacandra is an outsourced long-term investment practice for individuals, families, and trusts setting capital aside for retirement or legacy. There is no pooled fund. Each client's portfolio is held separately, in their own name, and managed to a single standard: buy what is worth more than it costs, then have the patience to let that be proven right.

R. Fenwick Vane Managing Partner & CIO
How capital is handled

Three things this firm won't compromise on.

Opportunism
Capital is put to work wherever value shows up — equities, credit, special situations — and just as readily left in cash when nothing on offer is worth owning.
Patience
Positions are sized and held for a full cycle, not a quarter. Sitting still, and sitting in cash, are both treated as decisions — not as inaction.
Alignment
Most of the manager's own liquid net worth sits in the same strategy, on the same terms, as every client account — no separate deal for the house.
